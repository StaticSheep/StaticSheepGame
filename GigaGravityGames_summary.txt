Giga Gravity Games (4-player, couch-play, arena brawler, arcade)

"In the future, there is only entertainment!" Giga Gravity Games is a 4-player, couch-play brawler set in a zero gravity arena. Up to 4 players duke it out for fame, money, and chips
as they try to survive the spectacle that is THE GRAVITY GAMES! Take control of either the green Ninja-bot, the red Ruiser-bot, the purple Magus-bot, or Steve-bot the saftey blue robot.
Test your reflexes and skills against your friends as your battle to survive the areana, and win Giga Chips from the spoiles of battle!
